Returning to Dublin from Glendalough, we made sure to pass through Glencullen and visit Johnnie Fox's Pub. The place promotes itself as the highest pub in Ireland (and one of the oldest), but that is not why I went, loved it, and recommend it. Yes, they don't shy away from the tourist buck, but, still, it lived up to my hosts' promises of a real Irish pub.

The building, the food (especially the "mountain chowder"), the music, and the Guinness. All great.
